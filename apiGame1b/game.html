How To Play

1.) Start a new game by clicking the "Get Question" button

2.) Read the generated question and select from the multiple choice answers provided

3.) Click the "Submit" button and see how you did

4.) Repeat until you get to 10 questions, then press the "New Game" button to get the results and start a new game
